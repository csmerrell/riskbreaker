## Origins
2 available origins:

### Astrologian
The stars flicker and fade. Your conviction to unravel the cause does not.

Dialogue flavor: Academic
Unique passive: Distill Light 
Starting weapon: Tome
Starting skill: Starflash

### Riskbreaker
Darkness creeps tighter by the night. You will stop its advance.

Dialogue flavor: Valorous
Unique passive: Challenge the Odds
Starting weapon: Sword & Shield
Starting skill: Shield Bash
